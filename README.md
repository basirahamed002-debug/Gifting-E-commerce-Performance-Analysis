# Gifting Industry Sales & Customer Analytics

## üìå Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data from a gifting platform to uncover trends in customer purchasing behavior, regional demand, and operational efficiency. By examining datasets across customers, products, and orders, this analysis identifies high-performing categories (like Soft Toys and Sweets) and peak seasonal demand periods (Valentine's Day, Raksha Bandhan, etc.).

## üìä Key Insights & Features
* **Revenue Analysis:** Calculation of total revenue across different product categories and occasions.
* **Temporal Trends:** Breakdown of sales by Month and Day of the week to identify peak shopping windows.
* **Geographic Distribution:** Analysis of order volume across various Indian cities (e.g., Aizawl, Bharatpur, Rajkot).
* **Logistics Tracking:** Measuring the efficiency of deliveries by calculating the difference between order and delivery timestamps.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Data-driven insights into customer demographics and spending habits.

## üìÇ Dataset Description
The project utilizes four primary datasets:
1.  **Orders:** Detailed transaction logs including Order ID, Product ID, Quantity, Order/Delivery dates, and Location.
2.  **Products:** Catalog information containing Product Names, Categories (Cake, Plants, Mugs, etc.), and Prices.
3.  **Customers:** Demographic data including Name, City, Gender, and Contact Information.
4.  **Summary Analytics:** Aggregated data showing average customer spending (~4,652 INR) and total revenue benchmarks.
